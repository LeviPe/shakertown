Take a tour of Shakertown, Kentucky

Shakertown, Kentucky is located about 20 miles southwest of Lexington. It contains 3,000 acres of land which provide the perfect place to explore the landscape of Central Kentucky with its 36 miles of trails and artifacts of past Shaker life.

 

Purpose

This project is intended to provide a visualization of the Shaker Village preservation along with the wildlands that surround it. Information that would be useful to those using the hiking trails will be provided, such as contour maps and views from particular trails.
